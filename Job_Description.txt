Job Title: Data Analyst – Entry Level
Company: InsightGen Technologies
Location: Remote / Hybrid (Bangalore, India)

Job Description:
We are looking for a passionate and detail-oriented Data Analyst to join our growing analytics team. You will be responsible for gathering, cleaning, analyzing, and visualizing data to help inform business decisions.

Responsibilities:
Collect and analyze large datasets from various sources (Excel, SQL, APIs)

Clean and preprocess data for accuracy and consistency

Build dashboards and visual reports using tools like Power BI, Tableau, or Excel

Present actionable insights to stakeholders

Work closely with product, marketing, and engineering teams

Required Skills:
Proficiency in Python (pandas, NumPy, matplotlib/seaborn)

Knowledge of SQL for data querying

Strong analytical and problem-solving skills

Good communication and storytelling with data

Familiarity with Excel (VLOOKUP, Pivot Tables)

Preferred:
Experience with Power BI or Tableau

Exposure to machine learning or predictive analytics

Understanding of business KPIs and A/B testing

Qualifications:
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field

0–2 years of experience in a data-related role
